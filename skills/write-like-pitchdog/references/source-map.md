## Coverage limits

The handover is excellent evidence for public brand and website writing. It is
not a complete corpus of Em, Jen, and Kay's natural emails, social posts, client
feedback, or internal chat. Guidance for those media transfers the observed
principles conservatively; it does not claim forensic imitation of an
individual.

The client-deck model is similarly split:

- observed: pitch.dog's explicit writing craft—one job per slide, controlled
  sequence, claim-led headlines, proof after attention;
- required judgement: the client's project voice must replace pitch.dog's
  surface personality.

## Public-sharing boundary

Do not bundle the raw handover, client quotes, gated personal history, private
assets, or stale commercial facts with a public version of this skill. Keep the
derived rules and a small non-sensitive calibration corpus only.
